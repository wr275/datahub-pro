## About DataHub Pro

DataHub Pro is the analytics layer every finance and operations team deserves. We take the Excel files that businesses already live in — budgets, forecasts, operational data, pipelines — and turn them into live KPI dashboards, pivot views, forecasting models, and NPV calculators without the user needing a BI tool or a data team.

We're a UK-based SaaS (datahubpro.co.uk) at the start of our commercial journey. The product is built, deployed, and already in the hands of early users. One thing is missing: someone to go out, turn that into a pipeline, and tell us what the market actually wants.

That's you.

---

## The Role

This is the **first commercial hire** at DataHub Pro. You'll report directly to the founder and own everything between *"we have a product"* and *"we have paying customers."*

You'll wear multiple hats — outbound prospecting, demos, closing, onboarding, relationship building, content. Over time, as we grow, you'll get to pick which hat you want to keep and hire into the rest.

This is not a seat on a bus that's already moving. It's a co-pilot role on a plane we're still building parts of mid-flight. If that energises you, read on.

---

## What You'll Actually Do

- **Outbound prospecting.** Build target account lists (UK SMBs and mid-market finance/operations teams), run LinkedIn and email outreach, book qualified demos.
- **Demos and closing.** Run product demos, follow up persistently, negotiate commercial terms, and close deals.
- **Onboarding and account management.** Get new customers to their first "aha" moment, keep them happy, and turn them into referrals. Early customers are gold — their feedback shapes the roadmap.
- **Marketing push.** Write LinkedIn content, help produce case studies, experiment with landing pages and messaging. You'll shape positioning alongside the founder.
- **Feedback loop.** Tell the founder what prospects are *actually* saying — what they love, what confuses them, what they'd pay more for. This is as valuable as the revenue you bring in.

## First 90 Days

**Days 1-30 — Absorb and set up.**
Learn the product cold. Shadow founder demos. Build a 200-account target list. Set up outbound tooling (CRM, sequencing). Start the first outbound cadence.

**Days 30-60 — Own the top of funnel.**
Run your first demos end-to-end. Book 15-20 qualified meetings per month. Bring back clear, structured signal on messaging, pricing, and which features close deals.

**Days 60-90 — Close and compound.**
Close your first 3-5 deals independently. Produce the first published case study. Propose your own GTM experiments based on what you've learned.
